Meet Your Guide

I'm Meenakshi.
I teach yoga to help you come home to yourself.

I've spent over eight years exploring the quiet wisdom of yoga — not just as postures, but as a way of living. Through every breath and every session, I guide you to listen inward, move with intention, and find stillness even in the midst of life's chaos.

Whether you're just beginning or deepening an existing practice, my classes are designed to meet you exactly where you are — with warmth, patience, and care.
